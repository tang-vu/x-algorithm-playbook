# Weekly Audit Checklist

> Run this audit every week to optimize your X strategy.

---

## Content Performance Review

### Top Performers

Review your best-performing posts this week:

- [ ] What type of content performed best? (thread, image, video, text)
- [ ] What hooks got the most engagement?
- [ ] What topics resonated?
- [ ] What time/day worked best?

### Underperformers

Review posts that flopped:

- [ ] Why did they underperform?
- [ ] Were hooks weak?
- [ ] Wrong timing?
- [ ] Off-topic for audience?

## Engagement Quality Audit

### Replies Given

- [ ] Did I engage meaningfully with others?
- [ ] Did I engage with people in my niche?
- [ ] Did I respond to all comments on my posts?

### Relationship Building

- [ ] Connected with any new people in my niche?
- [ ] Supported other creators?
- [ ] Any new DM conversations?

### Engagement Ratio

Target: 80% engaging / 20% posting

- [ ] Spent more time engaging than posting?
- [ ] Quality of engagement was high?

---

## Negative Signal Check

### Block/Mute Review

- [ ] Any signs of being blocked/muted? (sudden engagement drop)
- [ ] Posted anything controversial that might trigger blocks?
- [ ] Over-engaged with anyone (spam risk)?

### Content Safety

- [ ] All content was ToS compliant?
- [ ] No spam-like behavior?
- [ ] No engagement bait that could backfire?

---

## Posting Cadence Review

### Frequency

- [ ] How many posts this week? ___
- [ ] Was this sustainable?
- [ ] Quality maintained?

### Timing

- [ ] Posted at optimal times?
- [ ] Spaced posts 3+ hours apart?
- [ ] Available first hour for engagement?

### Consistency

- [ ] Posted on schedule?
- [ ] Maintained content pillars balance?
- [ ] Any days missed? Why?
